Our Projects

ATV Control System

Revolutionizing agricultural tasks with a rugged, remote-controlled ATV designed for harsh terrains and heavy loads. Enhance efficiency and safety in farming operations with our state-of-the-art control system.

Clients: Agricultural companies, large farms, and organizations involved in rural development and mechanized farming.

Building Energy Management System (BEMS)

Optimize energy usage in buildings with our advanced BEMS. Monitor, control, and reduce energy consumption efficiently, lowering costs while enhancing sustainability.

Clients: Commercial real estate developers, property management companies, and facility managers in large corporations.

Battery Management System (BMS)

Ensure the safety and longevity of Electric Tuk Tuk batteries with our intelligent BMS. Featuring real-time monitoring and advanced protection features, our BMS is key to efficient electric vehicle operation.

Clients: Electric vehicle manufacturers, especially those focusing on urban mobility solutions like electric Tuk Tuks, and companies involved in battery technology research.

Smart Temperature Controlled Box (STCB)

Transport sensitive materials with confidence using our STCB. Designed with precise temperature control and real-time monitoring, it ensures the safe delivery of critical goods.

Clients: Healthcare providers, pharmaceutical companies, and logistics companies specializing in the transport of perishable goods and sensitive medical supplies.
